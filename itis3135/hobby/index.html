What

Pokémon (short for "Pocket Monsters") is a multimedia franchise created by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures. It was first introduced in 1996 with the release of Pokémon Red and Green (later released as Red and Blue internationally) for the Game Boy. Since then, Pokémon has grown into one of the most successful and beloved franchises in the world, spanning video games, trading cards, television series, movies, and more.

Ways to enjoy Pokémon :

  • Mainline Games
  • Pokémon GO
  • The Trading Card Game (TCG)
  • Anime/Manga
  • Merchandise and Collectibles

When

Pokémon has been around for decades, evolving with new games, shows, and experiences. Pokémon has a national day on febuary 27th every year where they announce all the plans for the coming year. Here are some key moments:

A Simplified Timeline of Pokémons Biggest Achievements:

  • 1996: Pokémon Red & Green released in Japan.
  • 1998: Pokémon Red & Blue released internationally.
  • 1999: Pokémon Trading Card Game launched.
  • 2006: Pokémon Diamond & Pearl introduced online trading.
  • 2016: Pokémon GO revolutionized mobile gaming.
  • 2022: Pokémon Scarlet & Violet introduced open-world gameplay.

Where

Pokémon can be enjoyed in a variety of places, from digital platforms to real-world locations. Whether you're playing games, watching the anime, or participating in community events, Pokémon is accessible nearly everywhere.

Where to Play Pokémon:

  • Video Games: Available on Nintendo consoles like the Game Boy, DS, Switch, and mobile devices.
  • Pokémon GO: Played in real-world locations using augmented reality.
  • Pokémon Trading Card Game: Played in hobby shops, tournaments, and online through the Pokémon TCG Live app.

Where to Watch Pokémon:

  • TV & Streaming: Available on Netflix, Pokémon TV, and other streaming services.
  • Theatrical Releases: Pokémon movies are released in cinemas worldwide.

Where to Find Pokémon Communities:

  • Online Forums & Social Media: Reddit, Discord, Twitter, and Facebook groups.
  • Local Events: Pokémon tournaments, fan conventions, and community Pokémon GO meetups.

Why

Pokémon has remained one of the most beloved franchises for decades, and for good reason. It offers players and fans an immersive world filled with adventure, strategy, and creativity. But why do people love Pokémon so much?

Why People Love Pokémon:

  • Nostalgia & Longevity: Many fans grew up with Pokémon, and new generations continue to discover it.
  • Endless Adventure: The games let players explore vast regions, battle trainers, and catch Pokémon, making each journey unique.
  • Collecting & Strategy: Whether it’s completing the Pokédex, building a strong team, or mastering the Trading Card Game, Pokémon rewards skill and dedication.
  • Community & Social Interaction: Pokémon connects fans through online battles, local events, and cooperative gameplay in Pokémon GO.
  • Creative Expression: From fan art and theories to competitive play and team building, Pokémon allows players to express themselves in countless ways.

Why Pokémon is Still Popular:

Pokémon constantly evolves with new games, mechanics, and media while keeping the core experience fun and accessible. The franchise's ability to adapt while staying true to its roots is a major reason why it continues to capture hearts worldwide.

How

If you want to start playing the Pokémon games, I recommend trying some of the original DS games first. However, if you don’t own a DS and have a Nintendo Switch, I suggest starting with Pokémon Legends: Arceus for its fresh take on gameplay and open-world mechanics, followed by Pokémon Sword and Shield to experience the traditional battle system and competitive play.
